Why this doesn't contradict "delete your indicators"
Clutter isn't the tool count. It's the disagreement.

The actual problem with 14 indicators: they all measure something different and none of them agree. That's what makes a chart unreadable — not the number of lines on it.

What TradeKit does instead: four layers built to answer one question each — where are we in the bigger trend, where has price reacted before, is this the right moment, and how much am I risking. No layer contradicts another, because they're not each trying to predict the same thing.

Who this is for: discretionary traders who want one coherent read of the chart — not traders looking to add a 15th opinion to a chart that already has 14.
